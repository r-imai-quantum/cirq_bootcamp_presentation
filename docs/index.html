量子ビットに対する操作：量子ゲート¶

    • X NOT $$ X |0\rangle = |1\rangle \\ X |1\rangle = |0\rangle $$ $$ \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 1 \\ 1 & 0 \end{pmatrix} $$
  • ()
    • Z 1$(-1)$ $$ Z |0\rangle = |0\rangle \\ Z |1\rangle = -|1\rangle $$ $$ \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{pmatrix} $$
    • H (Hadamard) $$ H |0\rangle = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} |0\rangle + \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} |1\rangle\\ H |1\rangle = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} |0\rangle - \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} |1\rangle $$ $$ \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}} \begin{pmatrix} 1 & 1 \\ 1 & -1 \end{pmatrix} $$

  • Controlled-NOT (CNOT) $\textrm{CNOT}_{i,j}$

    $i$1$j$NOT

$$ \textrm{CNOT}_{0,1} |00> = |00> \\ \textrm{CNOT}_{0,1} |01> = |01> \\ $$

$$ \textrm{CNOT}_{0,1} |10> = |11> \\ \textrm{CNOT}_{0,1} |11> = |10> $$

  • Controlled-NOT (CNOT) $\textrm{CZ}_{i,j}$

    $i$1$j$Z

$$ \textrm{CZ}_{0,1} |00> = |00> \\ \textrm{CZ}_{0,1} |01> = |01> \\ $$

$$ \textrm{CZ}_{0,1} |10> = |10> \\ \textrm{CZ}_{0,1} |11> = -|11> $$

# Create a circuit.
circuit = cirq.Circuit(cirq.X.on(a) ** val, cirq.X.on(b) ** val)

# Display it.
print("Circuit with parameterized gates:\n")
print(circuit)
